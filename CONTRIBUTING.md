# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in this project.

## Open an issue first

**Please open an issue to discuss any proposed change before submitting a pull
request.** This repository is developed against a specific roadmap, and changes
are coordinated through issues. A PR that arrives without a prior, agreed-upon
issue will usually be closed regardless of its quality, simply because it
wasn't part of the plan.

## No unsolicited, automated, or AI-generated pull requests

**We do not accept unsolicited, automated, or AI-generated pull requests.
Please open an issue to discuss proposed changes before submitting a PR.**

To be explicit about what this means, because the distinction matters:

- ❌ **Not welcome:** automated submissions from bots or AI agents that scrape
  open issues and auto-generate "fixes" or PRs without being asked. These are
  closed unread, without explanation, and the account may be blocked. No offense
  is intended and none should be taken — they are simply out of scope for how
  this project is maintained.

- ✅ **Welcome:** real people who want to contribute, including people who use
  AI tools (Copilot, Claude, Cursor, etc.) to help write their code. Using an
  AI assistant to do your work is completely fine. The line is not "did a
  machine help you" — it's "is there a human who opened an issue, understands
  the change, stands behind it, and will respond to review."

If you are a person with an idea: open an issue, say hello, and let's talk. If
you are an automated agent: please don't open a PR here.

## How a contribution actually happens here

1. Open an issue describing the problem or proposal.
2. Wait for a maintainer to confirm it's in scope and agree on an approach.
3. Only then open a PR, referencing the issue.

Thank you for understanding.
